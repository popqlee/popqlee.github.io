Jianfeng Li (李剑峰)


Jianfeng Li is a Lecturer at Southwest University. He received his Ph.D. from Tottori University, advised by Prof. Shigang Li, and his MPhil degree from Sichuan University, advised by Prof. Yiguang Liu.

Research interests
  • Panoramic images and omnidirectional images (360° vision)
  • Representation and geometric understanding on spherical domains
  • Facial expression understanding
Students interested in these topics are welcome to contact me.
